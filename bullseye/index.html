BULLSEYE

UX/UI Design for Mobile Corneal Topographer Application • Summer 2016


WORK

Tasks
Research, Ideation, UX/UI Design

Tools
Adobe Photoshop, Marvel Prototyping App


SUMMARY

This was done as a part of Engineering The Eye 2016 hackathon conducted by L.V. Prasad Eye Institute and M.I.T. Media Lab.

Vision
Democratize corneal topography in low resource areas by developing a low-cost mobile attachment and application that works as a miniature corneal topographer, a device which maps the surface curvature of cornea.

Solution
Since we were working on a product that would be used by doctors — who spend most of their time taking critical decisions concerning the life of others, I designed the UX to be as non-intrusive to a doctor's workflow as possible by adopting a minimal style with a mellow colour scheme. A major consideration in this was to present map information lucidly.

Awards
Tej Kohli Corneal Institute (TKCI) Srujana Innovation Award and Award for Team Excellence.
