Work
Ideation, Illustration, Motion Design

Tools

“Fantasy is hardly an escape from reality. It's a way of understanding it.”

~ Lloyd Alexander



This is my token of appreciation for the universes I love with the help of a simple and minimal motion design project.
I wanted to keep it short and obviously, choosing 5 doors did not take much time.
Illustrating is fun, what's more exciting is making them come alive!
It really has a way of bringing that sparkle to our eyes by making us feel delighted and as happy as kids with their favourite toys. I hope I was able to do justice to that.
