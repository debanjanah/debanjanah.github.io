PROJECTS     ABOUT ME

Debanjana Haldar

Ultimately, it’s never about one but it’s about us, what’s around us, and how we strive to move forward.

I believe that a seemingly small design decision could enhance or diminish the crux of the experience a product is trying to achieve. Hence, I focus on the little details that go into making a product clutter free by combining user needs and motion design to positively impact user engagement.

With the agency of what I have and am able to give back, I want to bridge the gap between tools, platforms, resources and those who are in need of them. I have previously worked at places where I’ve had the benefit of meeting amazing people, learning from them and working together on products that address social needs and I wish to continue doing the same. Every step is a lesson, I cannot even fathom the immensity of the roads I’m excited to traverse; I’ve barely started the journey and I already want to run. I don’t think this will ever stop.

I'm currently pursuing engineering in Kolkata, India. I love travelling and if I’m ever in your locality, you will find me at your nearest tea-house sipping away while doodling or rhyming words. If you ever want to geek out over something you’re passionate about or just philosophize uncertainty in general, shoot me an email!

Thanks for stopping by!
