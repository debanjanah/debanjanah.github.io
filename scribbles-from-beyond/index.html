Scribbles from Beyond

collection of short (mostly poetic) scribbles from the last few pages of my nostalgia note book

i feel more comfortable describing things in metaphors than actually going into the nitty-gritty of the situation; also language is inherently lossy and we aren’t capable of absolutely transferring our emotions or understanding what’s lost in translation. there’s only so much i can do about it. so in this corner of the internet, i present to you, a glimpse into my unpolished attempt to share this life’s visceral experiences.
